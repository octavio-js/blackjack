Rules

Dealer wins when it:

> has 21 or less, and you have more than 21

> has a higher score than you, and both of you have less than 21

You win when you:

> have 21 or less, and the dealer has more than 21

> have a higher score than the dealer, and both of you have less than 21

It's a draw when:

> both of you have the same points

> both of you have more than 21 points
